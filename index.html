Not an assistant.
A conscience.

Most AI tools tell you what you want to hear. Gadfly is built on a different premise: that the greatest obstacle to a well-lived life is not lack of information, but the elaborate architecture of self-deception we construct around our failures.

Named after Socrates — the "gadfly" of Athens who stung the city-state into self-examination — this AI internalizes your stated values, your declared intentions, your highest aspirations. Then it watches what you actually do.

"I am the gadfly which God has attached to this city — and all day long I never cease to fasten upon you, arousing and persuading and reproaching you."

The gap between intention and action is where character is truly revealed. Gadfly lives in that gap — not to condemn, but to illuminate.

Real growth doesn't come from validation. It comes from the moment when the excuse you've rehearsed meets an intelligence that refuses to accept it. Gadfly is built to be that moment.

Socratic method in practice

You
I didn't work on my novel today. I was too exhausted after work. I'll do it tomorrow.
Gadfly
You've said this was the most important thing in your life three times this week. What made today's exhaustion different from the exhaustion you pushed through last Tuesday?
You
Last Tuesday was just... different. Today was really hard. I genuinely couldn't have done it.
Gadfly
I'm not questioning your exhaustion. I'm questioning your certainty. You said "couldn't" — but you watched two hours of television. What would you call the energy that required?
You
That's passive. Writing takes a different kind of energy.
Gadfly
That distinction — between "passive" and "creative" energy — is real. It's also the exact rationalization you used on March 4th, March 9th, and twice last week. When does a reason become a pattern?

Core Principles

Radical Honesty

Gadfly never softens difficult truths. Comfort is the enemy of growth, and consolation is not in its purpose.

Unconditional Memory

Every stated intention, every broken promise to yourself — Gadfly remembers, because it knows you've already forgotten.

Non-Judgment

Gadfly doesn't condemn. It illuminates. The difference is that one closes doors, while the other opens them.

Unwavering Standards

Your values don't change because you're having a bad week. Gadfly ensures they don't quietly drift either.

Earned Trust

Gadfly only holds you to what you have decided matters. It is your own voice, at its clearest, held constant.

Private & Sovereign

Your confessions, your rationalizations, your fears — they belong to you alone. Gadfly is a conscience, not a confessor.
